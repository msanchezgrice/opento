FAQ

Is Opento free?▾
Yes to join. We take a small fee from money you earn. A Pro plan later adds advanced automations.
Can I stop sharing data?▾
Yes. Revoke access instantly. Your consent ledger records every change.
Is this a job board?▾
No. It's your personal agent that brings qualified inbounds to you—under your rules.
How do payouts work?▾
Funds are escrowed on accept and released instantly when a task or interview completes.
How do you prevent spam?▾
Brands are verified, messages are relayed, and your Rep blocks anything outside your rules.
What can be shared?▾
Only what your recipe allows (e.g., role, city). Sensitive details like employer names stay hidden unless you explicitly allow them.
